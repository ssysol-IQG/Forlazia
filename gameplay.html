Gameplay

A complete fantasy adventure, not just a single encounter.

Every campaign moves through dangerous travel, tactical scenarios, discoveries, and the settlements where heroes recover and prepare.

Travel→Explore→Fight→Discover→Return→Grow

Exploration Turns

Search rooms, open doors, investigate discoveries, overcome hazards, and decide how far the party is willing to press forward.

Tactical Combat

Use positioning, weapon reach, ranged attacks, skills, spells, and cooperative timing to defeat dangerous foes.

Overland Travel

Navigate roads and wilderness, manage supplies, endure weather, and face events that make the journey part of the story.

Settlement Actions

Recover, trade, train, learn spells, improve equipment, gather information, and prepare for the next expedition.

Runic Shards

One resolution system, many kinds of challenge.

Attributes determine which Runic Shard die a hero rolls—from d6 through d20. The same intuitive progression supports attacks, exploration tests, resistance, and specialized actions.

Damage can explode on the highest result, creating dramatic moments without burying the game under layers of arithmetic.

Campaign Growth

Your party changes as the world opens around it.

Attributes & Skills

Improve the strengths that define a hero and unlock new ways to solve problems.

Treasure & Equipment

Discover weapons, armor, relics, potions, and socketed upgrades across multiple rarity tiers.

Magic & Soul Essence

Gather and spend Soul Essence to fuel divine, arcane, natural, and cosmic abilities.
