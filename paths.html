Paths of Forlazia

Callings shaped by discipline, belief, instinct, and power.

The paths of Forlazia are not rigid social classes. They are traditions, professions, philosophies, and ways of meeting a dangerous world.

Martial, Mystical & Learned Traditions

Ten paths through the Age of the Veilwake.

A person's lineage does not determine their calling. Any people may follow any path, and every path changes according to region, teacher, faith, and personal purpose.

The Master of the Battlefield

Warlord

A commanding tactician who shapes conflict through leadership, adaptation, morale, and coordinated action. Warlords are found among generals, clan leaders, rebel captains, caravan defenders, and rulers who lead from the front.

The Hand of Righteousness

Vindicator

A disciplined champion driven by conviction. Some serve a god, some a code, and others a deeply personal oath. Vindicators stand where belief must become action.

Nature’s Guardian

Warden

A hunter, protector, and master of difficult country. Wardens understand terrain, signs, creatures, and the responsibilities that come with defending a place from those who would exploit it.

The Beast of War

Savage

A warrior who draws upon endurance, instinct, and overwhelming physical force. The name describes a fighting tradition—not a lack of culture—and many Savages are protectors, clan champions, and survivors of brutal frontiers.

The Blade in the Shadows

Shadowblade

An infiltrator, spy, scout, or assassin who turns secrecy, timing, fear, and precision into power. Shadowblades often live at the edge of law, but not necessarily at the edge of loyalty.

The Voice of the Divine

Herald

A chosen intermediary who channels divine Aetherflow through devotion. A Herald’s miracles are inseparable from faith, doubt, duty, and the god whose power answers them.

Architect of the Arcane

Spellweaver

A scholar-practitioner who refines Aetherflow into structured sorcery. Spellweavers build repeatable patterns from forces that remain, at their heart, unstable.

Keeper of Forgotten Truths

Lorekeeper

A narrator, historian, decipherer, and seeker of echoes. Lorekeepers recover knowledge from ruins, relics, dead languages, and the Soul Essence of those who came before.

The Voice of the Untamed

Wildcaller

A mystic who communes with natural Aetherflow, beasts, storms, spirits, and living places. Wildcallers seek cooperation with nature rather than mastery over it.

Weaver of Dimensional Magic

Aetherbound

A defiant wielder of cosmic Aetherflow who reaches beyond gods toward stars, void, time, and distance. Their power inspires awe, suspicion, and accusations of heresy.

Power and Responsibility

Every path leaves a mark.

A Warlord may save a city and become its tyrant. A Herald may strengthen a god whose purpose they no longer trust. A Lorekeeper may recover a truth that should have remained buried. An Aetherbound may prove mortals no longer need divine permission—or tear open something they cannot close.

In Forlazia, power is never only capability. It is relationship, consequence, and memory.
