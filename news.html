First Chronicle

The Veil is waking.

For a century and a half, the people of Forlazia learned to live beneath the Veil. It shaped prayer and death, magic and omen, but remained distant enough to become ordinary.

That certainty is ending. Shards fall where no star crossed the sky. Forgotten temples answer names that have not been spoken in generations. Quiet fractures open like doors seen only from the corner of the eye.

The world has entered the Age of the Veilwake, and its history is once again being written.
