The World of Forlazia

A living world shaped by the Veil.

Ancient mystery, divine conflict, unstable magic, and hard-won hope define a land where reality has begun to thin.

The Veil

The event that changed everything.

Roughly a century and a half ago, the Veil transformed Forlazia. Magic changed, old powers stirred, and places once thought safe became touched by forces beyond ordinary understanding.

Now, subtle fractures are appearing—gate-like openings through which corruption, creatures, and impossible phenomena cross into the world.

Settlements

Villages, towns, and cities are more than places to shop. They hold stories, services, loyalties, and consequences that persist across the campaign.

Dangerous Wilds

Forests, mountains, deserts, rivers, swamps, tundra, and shattered lands each bring distinct travel risks and discoveries.

Forgotten Places

Ruined temples, crypts, collapsed strongholds, hidden roads, and sealed chambers preserve the secrets of earlier ages.

The Aether

The underlying force through which arcane, divine, natural, and cosmic powers flow.

The Pantheon

Gods and Veilborn powers exert influence through faith, relics, cults, visions, and contested miracles.

The Shards

Fragments connected to the Veil promise knowledge and power—but every discovery may carry a cost.

The Current Age

The Age of the Veilwake

The old wound is stirring. Shards are falling, fractures are widening, and those who understand what is happening are racing to shape what comes next.
