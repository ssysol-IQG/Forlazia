Heroes of Forlazia

Build the hero your party needs.

Race, archetype, attributes, equipment, skills, and advancement combine to create distinct roles and play styles.

The Five Featured Heroes

The first party to stand against the Veilwake.

The Warden

A precise elven hunter who controls distance, tracks threats, and turns the terrain into an advantage.

The Herald

A human leader who protects allies, inspires the party, and channels divine Aetherflow.

The Spellweaver

A winged Sylira master of arcane forces, battlefield control, and carefully managed Soul Essence.

The Savage

A towering Ravak warrior whose strength, durability, and furious assaults break enemy lines.

The Shadowblade

A halfling infiltrator who relies on speed, cunning, precision, and opportunistic strikes.

Your Own Champion

Mix race and archetype choices to shape a hero with a personal approach to combat, exploration, and survival.

The Peoples of Forlazia

Six races, each rooted in the world.

Humans, elves, dwarves, halflings, Sylira, and Ravak bring distinct strengths, histories, and perspectives to the party.

Race shapes a hero, but does not dictate their path. A Ravak may become a mystic, a Sylira may become a scout, and a halfling may stand at the center of a battle line.
